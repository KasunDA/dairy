Ordering Instructions

Milk is $10 per gallon. Please order as soon as you can after you get the Reminder... You will need to fill out the order form each week, as it’s not possible to keep track of “standing orders.” THE SPREADSHEET WITH ORDERS WILL BE SENT TO THE DAIRY ON TUESDAY AND THURSDAY EVENING, DEPENDING ON THE PICK UP DAY REQUESTED! If your name is not on the list by then, they may not have enough milk put aside for the Farm Shop milk group (you).

If you need to change your order, just use the order link again and put “Updated Order” in the comments box so we know what you want.

Milk can be picked up until 3 PM on the day you select. After 3 PM, all milk will be available to walk-ins, whether or not their name is on our list. If you aren’t going to be able to pick up your milk — or have someone pick it up for you—please give us a call (480-279-4474) so we can place it in the walk-in section available to anyone!.
